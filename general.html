Internet Service Providers

     Before you can get 'online' you must have a service which will provide you access to the World Wide Web via a phone line. There are many national and local companies to choose from. Cost for 'time' online is usually $20.00 a month for unlimited access.

Netcom
Netcom Online Communication Services is the Internet Communications Company providing services to businesses and productivity-seeking individuals worldwide.
AT&T WorldNet Service
The fastest growing ISP in the world, run by the largest telephone company in the world. Rated best ISP out of 500 by c|net.
Concentric Network
Large national ISP, very reliable, with access to most areas. Many smaller ISP's connect to the Internet using Concentric, like TDS Net.
TDS Net
Telephone and Data Systems, a national ISP that also happens to have local access to Athens, Tennessee.
CoCoCo.Net
Computerized Control Concepts, a local ISP with access to the Athens, Tennessee area.
Crystal Point
Crystal Point Internet Gateway, an ISP local to the Chattanooga area, with local access to the Athens, Tennessee area.
